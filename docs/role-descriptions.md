# Coach role descriptions 🌱

The 8 onboarding roles let coach pick quests that match your style. Each
role has a distinct vibe and a different mix of daily quests.

## 🌊 Yield Farmer
*"chasing every basis point"*
You optimize APY. Quests: deposit/withdraw across Kamino/Marinade, harvest
rewards, rebalance LP positions.

## 🎨 NFT Curator
*"vibes over P&L"*
You collect, mint, and trade NFTs. Quests: list on Tensor, bid on auctions,
spot floor price changes.

## 🏗 Builder
*"deploy first, polish later"*
You write and ship contracts. Quests: deploy a program, verify on Anchor,
test a CPI call.

## ⚡ Trader
*"speed over patience"*
You scalp on Jupiter. Quests: complete a swap, set a limit order, check
PnL on a closed position.

## 🛡 Risk Manager
*"sleep is alpha"*
You hedge and diversify. Quests: open a delta-neutral position, set
stop-losses, monitor liquidation buffers.

## 🌐 DePIN Operator
*"real hardware, real yield"*
You run hotspots and node infra. Quests: claim hotspot rewards, check
uptime, register a new node.

## 📊 Data Sleuth
*"on-chain detective"*
You read flow and trace movements. Quests: identify a whale tx, label a
suspicious wallet, follow a token through 3 hops.

## 🎲 Degen
*"max leverage, no regrets"*
You ape. Quests: open a perp position, mint a fresh meme coin, cap a
20× trade.

---

Don't worry about picking the *perfect* role on day 1 — you can switch
anytime in *Settings → Coach Style*, and your streak carries over.
