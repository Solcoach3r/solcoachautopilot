# Quest types 🎯

Today's quest is one of these eight categories. Coach picks based on your
chosen role and the current Solana ecosystem state.

## 🌊 Yield
Move assets between staking, lending, and LP positions. Examples:
- "Stake 0.5 SOL on Marinade"
- "Deposit 50 USDC into Kamino"
- "Harvest your Drift rewards"

## 🎨 NFT
Interact with the Metaplex / Tensor side. Examples:
- "Mint an open-edition NFT (devnet)"
- "List one of your NFTs on Tensor at floor +5%"
- "Unlock a hidden trait on Compressed NFT"

## 🏗 Build
Hands-on contract or tooling work. Examples:
- "Deploy a hello-world Anchor program"
- "Verify your program with `anchor verify`"
- "Make a CPI call from your test program"

## ⚡ Trade
Execute a swap or limit order. Examples:
- "Swap 1 USDC → SOL on Jupiter"
- "Set a limit order at SOL ≥ $200"
- "Close one of your open trades"

## 🛡 Hedge
Risk management activities. Examples:
- "Open a 1× short on Drift to hedge a 1 SOL spot"
- "Set a 5% stop-loss on your largest position"
- "Check liquidation buffer in Sentinel"

## 🌐 DePIN
Hardware-network interactions. Examples:
- "Claim your Helium hotspot rewards"
- "Boost a Hivemapper region for 1 day"
- "Submit a Render job (CPU-light test)"

## 📊 Read
On-chain detective work. Examples:
- "Identify the top 3 SOL whales by movement today"
- "Trace a token through 3 hops"
- "Label one suspicious wallet with a tag"

## 🎲 Play
For the truly degenerate. Examples:
- "Open a 5× perp position (1% notional max)"
- "Mint a fresh meme coin with intentional cap"
- "Try one of the prediction markets"

## How quests get scored

Each quest carries an XP value (roughly: difficulty × scarcity × on-chain
verifiability). Completing a quest also feeds your streak, level path,
and weekly calendar fill 🌟

> Don't see a quest type that matches your interest? Suggest it in
> *Settings → Coach Style → Suggest a category*.
